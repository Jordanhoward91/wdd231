Guided Hunting Tours in the Sawtooth Mountains

Explore the best hunting opportunities in the Sawtooth Mountains with expert guides. Whether you're hunting elk, deer, or upland birds, we offer guided tours that ensure safety, knowledge, and adventure.

Elk Hunting with Guide John

Experience the thrill of elk hunting in the heart of the Sawtooth Mountains with Guide John. John has over 15 years of experience guiding hunters through these rugged landscapes. He knows the best locations, tracking techniques, and how to handle various weather conditions, ensuring you have a safe and exciting hunt.

In addition to your hunt, John provides expert advice on elk behavior, how to approach the animals without spooking them, and essential safety protocols. All necessary permits and regulations are covered, making your adventure stress-free.

  • Duration: 3-5 days
  • Difficulty: Moderate to High
  • Best Time: October - November
  • Included: Guide services, gear, transportation, permits

Deer Hunting Adventures

Embark on a memorable deer hunting adventure with one of our knowledgeable guides in the Sawtooth Mountains. Our guides are experienced in both spot-and-stalk hunting and tree stand hunting, depending on your preferences and the area's terrain.

This guided tour includes comprehensive education on the best hunting methods for deer, as well as how to identify and track them. Our guides will help you navigate the forest, all while ensuring a safe and respectful approach to wildlife conservation.

  • Duration: 4-6 days
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Time: September - December
  • Included: Guide services, gear, transportation, permits

Bird Hunting Expeditions

Join our expert guides for an exciting upland bird hunting expedition in the Sawtooth Mountains. Whether you're hunting pheasants, grouse, or chukar, we offer guided tours that take you to the most abundant bird habitats in the region.

Our guides are well-versed in bird calls, field techniques, and how to handle your firearm safely. You'll also learn about local bird species and their migration patterns, making this a fun and educational experience for hunters of all skill levels.

  • Duration: 2-3 days
  • Difficulty: Low to Moderate
  • Best Time: September - November
  • Included: Guide services, dogs (for bird hunting), gear, transportation, permits

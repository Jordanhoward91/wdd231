Best Hiking Trails in the Sawtooth Mountains

Discover the beauty of the Sawtooth Mountains with some of the best hiking trails in the area. Whether you're looking for a challenging summit or a peaceful stroll, these trails offer something for everyone. Below is a list of top trails, each with a difficulty rating, distance, and some key highlights.

Alpine Lake Trail

Difficulty: Moderate | Distance: 4.5 miles | Elevation Gain: 1,200 ft

This scenic trail takes you to a serene alpine lake surrounded by towering peaks. Perfect for an intermediate hike, you'll be rewarded with breathtaking views of the Sawtooth Range.

Redfish Lake Loop

Difficulty: Easy | Distance: 3.2 miles | Elevation Gain: 500 ft

A leisurely hike around Redfish Lake offering stunning reflections of the Sawtooth Mountains on the water. Ideal for beginners or families with young children.

Difficulty: Hard | Distance: 7.5 miles | Elevation Gain: 3,000 ft
